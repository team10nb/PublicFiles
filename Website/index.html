Animation of Sorting Algorithms and their Correctness

The project, developing an open-sourced software, which helps students visualize and understand sorting algorithms and their correctness, is promising. It has a potential and broad market which is focusing on helping people understand and be interested with the sorting algorithm. Nowadays computer science has become a popular subject to society, so this software may be an entry level for the newcomers.
